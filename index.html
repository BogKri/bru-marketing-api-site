Service Status: LIVE

About This Application

BRU Marketing API is an internal marketing tool developed by BRU AG. It connects to the Meta (Facebook & Instagram) platform via the Marketing API to help our team manage and analyze social media marketing activities efficiently.

What This App Does

  • Marketing Analytics: Retrieves and analyzes ad campaign performance data, including impressions, reach, engagement metrics, and spend across Facebook and Instagram.
  • Comment Management: Reads and manages comments on our business pages and ad posts to ensure timely and consistent community engagement.
  • AI-Assisted Responses: Uses AI to help draft and manage responses to customer comments and inquiries on our social media channels.
  • Campaign Monitoring: Monitors active campaigns and provides alerts and reports on performance changes.

Permissions Used

This application requests access to the following Meta API permissions in order to function:

  • pages_read_engagement — To read engagement data on our pages.
  • pages_manage_metadata — To manage page settings and metadata.
  • pages_read_user_content — To read user-generated content such as comments and reviews.
  • pages_manage_engagement — To respond to and manage comments on our pages.
  • ads_read — To read advertising campaign data and insights.
  • ads_management — To manage ad campaigns and related settings.

Data Usage & Privacy

BRU Marketing API is used exclusively by BRU AG for internal business purposes. We do not share, sell, or distribute any data obtained through the Meta API to third parties. All data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection regulations, including the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
